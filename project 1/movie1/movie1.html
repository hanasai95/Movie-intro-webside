Jojo Rabbit (2019)

Storyline

A World War II satire that follows a lonely German boy named
Jojo (Roman Griffin Davis) whose world view is turned upside down
when he discovers his single mother (Scarlett Johansson) is hiding a
young Jewish girl (Thomasin McKenzie) in their attic. Aided only
by his idiotic imaginary friend, Adolf Hitler (Taika Waititi), Jojo
must confront his blind nationalism. Written by Fox Searchlight
Pictures.
