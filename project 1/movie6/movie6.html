Shoplifters (2018)

Storyline

A 2018 Japanese drama film directed, written and edited by Hirokazu
Kore-eda. Starring Lily Franky and Sakura Ando, it is about a non-
biological family that relies on shoplifting to cope with a life of
poverty. Kore-eda wrote the screenplay contemplating what makes a family, and
inspired by reports on poverty and shoplifting in Japan. Principal
photography began in mid-December 2017.
