2046 (2004)

Storyline

The story evolves around the main character, Zhou Mo Wan who writes
a novel about a mysterious train that leaves for a place called 2046
every once in a while. Everyone who boards that train has the same
intention - which is to recapture their lost memories. It is said that in
2046, nothing ever changed. Nobody knows for sure if it was true,
because nobody who went there had ever come back- except for
one. He had been there but He chose to leave. He wanted to change.
